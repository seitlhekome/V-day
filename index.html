In Every Chapter, It Is You

“Some loves are loud. Ours feels like a quiet library — warm, timeless, and sacred.”
To Minah Lekau,

Loving you feels like discovering a rare book — one I want to read slowly, carefully, understanding every word, every thought, every dream. I admire the way your mind works — the depth of your thoughts, the seriousness with which you approach your research, the quiet strength behind your ambition. I cannot wait to see you stand in front of a lecture hall, speaking with confidence, shaping minds, building a legacy through knowledge. And I promise this — while you chase your masters, your research, your calling, I will be beside you. Supporting. Encouraging. Loving. One day, after long academic days, we will travel — to old cities with cobbled streets, to rustic cabins with wooden furniture and soft silence, to places where the only thing louder than the wind is our laughter. I do not just love you for today. I love you for the life we are quietly building.
Always yours.
In every future I imagine — it is you walking beside me. And that is enough.
